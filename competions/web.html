Web Designing Competition

Show off your skills and win big!

Event Details

  • Entry Fee: ₹50 per participant
  • Winner's Prize: ₹2000

Instructions to Participate

1. Registration

  • Register online or at the venue with a fee of ₹50.
  • Upon successful registration, you’ll receive your Participation ID.

2. Competition Rules

  • Participants will be assigned a theme or concept at the beginning of the competition.
  • Design a website based on the provided theme within the allotted time (e.g., 2-3 hours).
  • Use of external libraries or frameworks is permitted but must be disclosed.

3. Event Format

  • Participation can be individual or team-based (maximum 2 members per team).
  • Necessary equipment, including computers with software like Visual Studio Code, Notepad++, and browsers, will be provided.
  • Participants may use HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and any frontend or backend technology they prefer.

Judging Criteria

  • Creativity & Design: How visually appealing and innovative the design is.
  • Functionality: Usability and responsiveness of the website.
  • Relevance: Alignment of the design with the given theme.
  • Code Quality: Neatness and efficiency of the written code.

Winner Announcement

The winner with the highest overall score will receive ₹2000. Judges' decisions will be final and binding.

Important Notes

  • Arrive 30 minutes before the competition begins.
  • Optional: Bring your own USB drives or external storage for backups.
  • Follow the event coordinator's instructions and maintain decorum during the competition.
