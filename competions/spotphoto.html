Sports Photography Competition Guidelines

Capture the action, freeze the motion, and show off your photography skills!

Event Details

  • Entry Fee: ₹50 per participant
  • Winner's Prize: ₹1000

Instructions to Participate:

  1. Registration:
    • Register online or at the venue with a fee of ₹50.
    • Upon registration, you’ll receive your participation ID.
  2. Competition Rules:
    • Participants must submit a single photograph related to sports.
    • The photo must be taken during the tech fest (if applicable) or from a prior sports-related event.
    • Basic editing is allowed (e.g., brightness, contrast adjustments), but heavily edited or composite images will not be accepted.
  3. Submission Guidelines:
    • Submit your photo in digital format (JPEG/PNG).
    • File size should not exceed 10 MB.
    • Submission deadline: [Specify date and time].
    • Email your entry to your-email@example.com with your name and registration ID.
  4. Judging Criteria:
    • Composition: Creativity and framing of the shot.
    • Emotion & Action: How well the photo captures the spirit of sports.
    • Technical Quality: Clarity, lighting, and focus.
    • Originality: Uniqueness of the captured moment.
  5. Winner Announcement:
    • The photo with the highest score based on the judging criteria will win ₹1000.
    • Judges’ decisions will be final and binding.
  6. Important Notes:
    • Participants must use their own cameras or smartphones for photography.
    • Photos must be original and not downloaded or plagiarized.
    • Maintain the event's decorum and avoid disrupting sports activities while capturing photos.
